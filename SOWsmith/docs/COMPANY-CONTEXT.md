# Company context — SOWsmith (fictional sample company)

> **SOWsmith is a fictional company** invented purely as the sample tenant for this POC. Any resemblance to
> a real organization is coincidental. **All names, prices, quantities, reviewer emails and documents in the
> knowledge base are illustrative and synthetic — no real or confidential company data is used.**

This page describes the fictional company the knowledge base, the agent's system prompt, and the demo are
contextualized to, so the bid packages read like a real energy/industrial contractor's.

## Who SOWsmith is
- **What it does:** SOWsmith delivers specialized **asset maintenance, construction, and contracting
  services** to the energy, chemical, manufacturing, and industrial sectors.
- **Posture:** safety-first, governed by **OSHA, DOT, and PHMSA** (pipeline) requirements — reflected
  throughout the knowledge base and the agent's system prompt.

## Sectors served
Oil & Gas — Facilities, Pipelines, Terminals · Gas Processing & Treatment · CO₂ · Refining & Chemicals ·
Renewables (solar / wind) · Manufacturing & Industrial.

## Service lines
Engineering & Design · Procurement · Construction · Maintenance · Measurement & Fabrication ·
Cathodic Protection · Electrical & Instrumentation · Pipeline Integrity · Field Services — spanning the full
asset lifecycle (new construction, upgrades, commissioning & start-up, ongoing maintenance and rehabilitation).

## Why this maps to the bid-package use case
SOWsmith's Supply Chain function issues **Statements of Work (SOW)** and **Requests for Proposal (RFP)** to
subcontractors and vendors across these field disciplines. The POC's **12 bid-package service types** map
directly onto these service lines and a realistic project mix:

| Bid-package service type | Service line / context |
|---|---|
| Pipeline Construction · Welding Services | Onshore pipeline construction |
| Facility Maintenance · Civil & Earthwork | Construction & maintenance of facilities / sites |
| Electrical & Instrumentation | E&I service line |
| Cathodic Protection · Coating & Insulation | Corrosion protection |
| Pipeline Integrity | Pipeline integrity & regulatory testing |
| Fabrication | Skid-mounted measurement & fabrication |
| Hydrotesting & Commissioning | Commissioning & start-up |
| Station & Terminal | Meter/measurement stations, terminals, processing facilities |
| Demolition & Abandonment | Pipeline/facility decommissioning & rehabilitation |

> **Data note:** generated bid packages leave prices and non-standard commercial/legal terms as **bracketed
> placeholders** by design — the agent never invents commercial figures, and a human approves every package.
